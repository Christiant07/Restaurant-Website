Plain Protein Pizza

This Protein Power Pizza is a high-protein, fitness-focused pizza designed for active individuals looking to fuel their workouts while enjoying a delicious meal. Made with a protein-enriched crust and topped with lean ingredients, it delivers both flavor and performance.

Ingredients:

  • Plain Greek Yogurt
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt, Garlic Powder, and Italian Seasonings
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Substitutions and Variations:

  • Regular all-purpose flour
  • Non-dairy plain Greek Yogurt
  • Dairy-free Cheese

Steps to make Plain Protein Pizza

  1. Make the self-rising flour by mixing the flour, baking powder, salt, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the Greek yogurt and use your hands or a fork to mix everything together until a smooth dough ball forms (about 2–3 minutes).
  3. Spread the dough onto a greased pizza pan or baking sheet. Bake at 400°F for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from the oven and add tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Bake for an additional 15–17 minutes.
  5. Let the pizza cool for 5 minutes, then slice and serve! Gainz!!!!
